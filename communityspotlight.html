significant role in advocating for equal, inclusive, and quality education for all marginalized
and underprivileged children. Aside from the key people who contributed remarkably, this will
also serve as an avenue to openly share inspiring and motivating stories from our beneficiaries
that have graduated and succeeded in overcoming difficult struggles in
their educational journey.
