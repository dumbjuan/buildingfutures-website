Where Do Your Donations Go?
Did you know?
  • 9 out of 10 Filipino children under age 10 cannot read and understand a simple story.
  • The Philippines ranks among the lowest-performing countries in global learning assessments.
  • From classroom shortages to under-supported teachers, continue to leave millions of learners behind.
Your donation directly supports:
  • Children in marginalized communities across Davao City, including Indigenous Peoples (IPs), students with disabilities (PWD) and students with limited access to education.
  • Scholars and learners who need better tools, resources, and support systems to thrive.

Together, we’re investing in:

  • Early Childhood Foundations- Combating malnutrition and improving access to high-quality early childhood care and development.
  • Education for Marginalized Communities & IPs - In collaboration with partner non-organizations such as Kalayag, providing learning materials and educational resources to IP students.
  • Quality Learning & Resources - Supplying schools and students with tools for effective learning
  • Teacher Empowerment & System Strengthening- Building capacity, collaboration, and national action to improve the state of education in the Philippines.
